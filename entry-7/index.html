I love pizza! and NYC is the perfect place for a pizza lover!

There are pizza places at almost every corner in New York City, so it is very easy to get confused about what to try… Well let me help you out! This website lists some of the pizza places that I have tried out with my ratings! Click on the images to get redirected to the pizza place's website.

1. Joe's Pizza

Unique to New York (save for one location in Michigan), this is the slice people think of when they think of a New York slice. Large, foldable and cinematically cheesy, it's a huge part of the local pizza tapestry. I really like the sauce and cheese ratio of the pizza. My rating would be a 4/5 stars. 2. Grimaldis

This pizzeria has been around for several decades, and it’s famous for the blistered and chewy pies coming out of its coal-fired oven. 4.1/5 stars. Click on the stars and leave a review!

★★★★★

3. Artichoke's Pizza

This East Village classic is perfect for fulfilling late night cravings. The thick square slices have a cripsy bite into them, though can be very heavy eats. 3.7/5 stars. Click on the stars and leave a review!

★★★★★

4. Di Fara Pizza

Yes, you may have to wait for more than an hour to try this Midwood institution, but a trip to the original standard-setting shop founded by the late Domenico DeMarco in 1965 is essential for pizza lovers and dabblers everywhere. Slices and whole pies are both available. 4/5 stars. Click on the stars and leave a review!
