My favourite places in Sri Lanka!

I used to live in Sri Lanka for 6 years from 2016 to 2022 and have amazing and happy memories of living on the island. Through this website, I wanted to explore my favorite places on the island. Sri Lanka is a land of breathtaking beauty, diverse landscapes, and a rich cultural tapestry waiting to be explored. Nestled in the Indian Ocean, Sri Lanka, often referred to as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a destination that promises travelers a unique and unforgettable experience.

1. Mirissa: It is a serene coastal town located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka, and it's known for its stunning beaches that offer golden sand and crystal-clear waters, tranquil atmosphere, and vibrant marine life (it is popular for whale watching).
